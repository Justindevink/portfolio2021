MultiGether

MultiGether was a concept for a project I did for the minor Visual Interface Design, where people could find games to play with eachother online. Since we were all sitting at home due to the pandemic, I tried to make the interface fitting for non-gamers, so that they could find co-op games with different difficulty levels to play with their friends. The user could also search by popularity and amount of players and save them in a playlist that is shared across their group of friends.
