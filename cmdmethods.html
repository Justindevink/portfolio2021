CMD Methods Pack

At the education Communication & Multimedia Design we often use a set of cards, called the CMD Methods Pack, which can help you find the proper research methods for your projects. As an internship project, I redesigned the CMD Methods Pack website, together with 3 other CMD students. We also added a new function where users could make new projects and add cards to them. The user could add different sections in their projects to store cards in and lastly the user could rename those sections into week numbers or the name of design phases.

  • Year: 2020
  • Client: HvA
  • Role: UX / Visual Designer & Front End Developer
