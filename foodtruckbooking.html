Foodtruck Booking

With the Foodtruck Booking app, a user could rent or hire Foodtrucks for events like musicfestivals or foodfestivals. This project focused a lot on Human Computer Interaction and Information Architecture. Context flavours were also an important focus point of this project. Lastly, the interface was designed specifically for users who wanted to rent and user who wanted to hire a foodtruck. For instance, users who wantend to hire a foodtruck, saw different kinds of foodtruck suggestions and could contact users who rent a foodtruck. While users users who rented a foodtruck could sign up for festivals if they were in need of foodtrucks.
