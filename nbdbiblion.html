NBD Biblion Booksearcher

NBD Biblion Booksearcher was a concept where young visitors of the NBD Biblion library could find available books by answering a couple of questions about what kind of books they're interested in. This project focused a lot on user flow and microinteractions. The user could add books to their wishlist and see where their added books were stored inside of the library. Lastly the user could mail or print their wishlist so that they could keep their wishlist while searching for the desired books.

  • Year: 2018
  • Client: NBD Biblion & HvA
  • Role: UX / Visual Designer
