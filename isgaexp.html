ISGA Exp

ISGA Exp. was my first individual project where I designed a concept for an app that international students in Amsterdam could use to explore the city and meet other international students. This app served as a companion app for the International Student Guide Amsterdam (ISGA), a book that is made yearly by HvA Students, where international students can learn about interesting places in Amsterdam. When the user saw an interesting place that they wanted to visit, the app could show them the quickest but also safest route to that location. While exploring the city, the user could see points of interest on a map created specifically for international students, like places to study, places to eat or do groceries and of course monuments etc.

  • Year: 2018
  • Client: HvA
  • Role: UX Designer
