Mountain Step

Mountain Step is a concept for an app and website that hikers could use to plan their trips and routes. This project focused on C-strategies and in what fase of the trip the user used the app and website. For instance, the ability to plan your routes and trip at home on the website and using the app to make the trip.

  • Year: 2019
  • Client: HvA
  • Role: UX Designer
