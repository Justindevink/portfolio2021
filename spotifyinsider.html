Spotify Insider

Spotify Insider was a concept that could be used at a music festival when visitors waited at a stage for the artist to perform. This is probably the most boring part of a music festival, mostly for the biggest fans who sometimes wait for hours at an empty stage, just so they can stand at the front of the crowd. This is when the stage can be used as a big screen for a quiz about the artist who is about to perform. Users can participate by simply opening the Spotify app on their phones and scanning the QR-code displayed on the stage. By answering question correctly, the festival visitor could win prizes like exclusive merch, snippets of unreleased music or even acces to the golden circle of the performance and a short meet and greet.
