Corona in your studentdays

Corona in your studentdays was a team project where we wanted to bring attention to how the daily lives of students have been changed during the coronavirus pandemic through data visuals, with data collected from Statistics Netherlands (CBS). Our focus for this project was to bring awareness to how the lives of students have been changed outside of their studies, like how many friends did student see throughout 2020 or could they still practice their favorite sport? We wanted to give our personal opinions as a team of students about these matters by adding a groupchat to the data visuals where we discussed the topics treated in this project.
